Upload Dental Photos
Two photos of the side of your dog's mouth — these are the most diagnostically important views.

📸 How to get a good photo

  1. Position your dog in good lighting (outdoors or near a window)
  2. Gently pull the cheek/lip back with one finger to expose the back teeth
  3. Aim for the large pointed tooth at the back — that's the key tooth
  4. Make sure the pink gumline above the teeth is visible
  5. Hold your phone horizontal and use flash if needed
